AI Interview Overview

A smart interview solution that leverages LLMs, voice AI, and deep learning to automate, evaluate, and streamline candidate screening and assessment.

üìå System Overview Diagram

üß© Background

Traditional recruitment requires significant human effort in CV screening and conducting interviews. It is time-consuming, subjective, and difficult to scale. This project proposes a fully automated AI interview platform that simulates HR interactions using advanced language and speech models.

üöÄ Project Goals

  • Replace initial human interviews with a fully autonomous voice-based system.
  • Provide consistent and unbiased candidate evaluation.
  • Enable one-click integration for HR teams and recruiters.

üõ†Ô∏è Key Features

  • üìÑ CV parsing and JD-based question generation.
  • üó£Ô∏è Natural two-way interview using Google TTS/STT APIs and LLMs.
  • üéØ Intelligent question branching based on candidate answers.
  • üìä Automated scoring and evaluation reports.
  • üß† Anti-cheating logic with randomized access and dynamic content.
  • üóÉÔ∏è Candidate management dashboard with full logs and transcripts.

üî¨ System Architecture

The platform includes a frontend interview interface (Svelte), a backend logic and scoring engine (FastAPI + Python), speech services (Google APIs), and a backend database for storing candidate sessions and evaluations. LLMs (e.g., GPT or Mistral) are used for both question generation and answer analysis.

üß™ Technologies Used

  • Frontend: Svelte, TailwindCSS
  • Backend: FastAPI, Python, PostgreSQL
  • AI Modules: OpenAI / Mistral APIs, Sentence Transformers
  • Speech: Google TTS, Google STT

üìä Expected Impact

  • Reduce HR workload by 70% for first-round interviews.
  • Improve candidate fairness by eliminating bias in early screening.
  • Increase scalability for startups and enterprises with large applicant pools.
